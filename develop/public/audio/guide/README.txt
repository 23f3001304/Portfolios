Drop guide voice clips here to replace the built-in synth (CC0 / royalty-free,
e.g. from pixabay.com/sound-effects or mixkit.co). Supported: .mp3, .wav, .ogg.

The studio guide looks for these names (all optional):

  blip   - one short voice blip, played per syllable while talking.
           It is pitch-shifted per character automatically, so one clip
           is enough to make whole sentences sound melodic.
  ooh, ahh, uhh, umm, hmm - short vowel "filler" sounds, played at the
           start of a line and at each comma/period pause.

Anything missing just falls back to the synth, so partial sets are fine.
A good start: a single "blip.mp3" (a tiny cartoon/voice pop).
